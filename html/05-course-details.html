Course Description

Overview:
This comprehensive Health & Safety Fundamentals course provides essential knowledge and practical skills required for all employees working in government office environments. The course covers risk assessment, emergency procedures, workplace safety regulations, and personal responsibility for maintaining a safe working environment.

Learning Objectives:
By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Identify common workplace hazards and risks
  • Understand emergency evacuation procedures
  • Apply basic first aid principles
  • Report safety incidents and near misses
  • Use office equipment safely and ergonomically
  • Understand legal responsibilities under health and safety legislation

Target Audience:
All employees, mandatory for new starters within first month of employment. Annual refresher required.

Prerequisites:
None

Course Content

Module Title Type Duration Description
